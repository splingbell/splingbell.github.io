Privacy Policy

Splingbell (hereinafter referred to as the "Developer") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of user information in the application "Animal Restaurant" (Japanese title: あにまるレストラン) (hereinafter referred to as the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

However, the App may automatically collect the following information:

  • App usage data (launch frequency, gameplay progress, operation history, etc.)
  • Device information (operating system, device type, language settings, etc.)
  • Information required for ad display and reward delivery
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing, operating, and improving the App
  • Analyzing usage trends and improving quality
  • Investigating and fixing bugs or technical issues
  • Displaying advertisements and granting in-app rewards

3. External Services Used

The App uses the following external services:

Firebase Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics, provided by Google LLC, to analyze usage data and improve the App. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect information related to app usage.

LevelPlay (Advertising)

The App uses LevelPlay, an advertising service provided by Unity Technologies and its partners, to deliver rewarded advertisements. For ad delivery, advertising identifiers and device-related information may be collected and used.

Please refer to the privacy policies of each service provider for details on how they handle collected information.

4. Provision to Third Parties

The Developer will not provide collected information to third parties, except when required by law.

5. Use by Minors

The App is not specifically designed for children. If minors use the App, they should do so with the consent of a parent or legal guardian.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. The revised Privacy Policy will take effect when posted within the App or on a related website.
